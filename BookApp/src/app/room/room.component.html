our rooms

The Columbia Hotel was originally five Victorian town houses which were combined to create one unique building. As a result all our bedrooms are uniquely sized, offering a diverse selection of accommodation to suit your needs. These range from single rooms to larger family rooms. Our park-facing rooms offer views into Kensington Gardens, while our front and back facing rooms offer a typically quieter stay.
